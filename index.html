Highest Vaccinated Countries

According to the WHO, vaccines produce protection against the disease, as a result of developing an immune response to the SARS-Cov-2 virus WHO also mentions that the COVID-19 vaccines are safe for most people 18 years and older, including those with pre-existing conditions of any kind, including auto-immune disorders.

As of October 2021, there are six approved vaccinations by the World Health Organization

They are, Pfizer-BioNTech, Moderna, Johnson & Johnson's, Oxford-Astrazeneca, Sinopharm and Sputnik V

United Arab Emirates, the highest vaccinated country, started vaccinating it's population in December 14th with the Sinopharm vaccine as soon as it was released for the general public.

Within a time span of around 10 months, UAE has vaccinated 94% of its population

The UAE is also noted for its global level efforts to provide assistance to countries in need.

For instance, the UAE together with other international partners, has provided over 500 Million syringes and needles required for the COIVID-19 vaccine
